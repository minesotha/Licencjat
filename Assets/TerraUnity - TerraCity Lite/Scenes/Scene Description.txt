This Demo Is Created By TerraLand, TerraTrees & TerraCity To Simulate Bibury Village, Gloucestershire, England


TerraCity

     - 393 Buildings Placed On Terrain
     - 8 Building Models Are Used To Simulate City
     - 1 TerraMap Used As Buildings-Map (3 Colors To Represent Simple, Complex & Misc Building Types)
     - Buildings Contain 8 "Facade" Textures With Normal Maps
     - Buildings Contain 12 "Roof" Textures
     - Buildings Contain 1 "Window" Texture

All Buildings Are Placed By TerraCity In 3 Placements With Various Settings Based On A TerraMap. All Buildings Have Been Textured By TerraCity "Texture Engine" During Placement. No Manual Editing Has Been Applied.


TerraTrees

     - 144628 Trees Placed On Terrain By TerraTrees
     - 6 Tree Prototypes (3 Trees & 3 Bushes) Are Used To Simulate Vegetation Zones
     - 1 TerraMap Used As Vegetation-Map (2 Colors To Represent Trees & Bushes)
     - 1 Bark/Leaves Texture Used For Each Tree With "Colors Variation" Feature

All Trees Are Placed By TerraTrees "Mapping Trees" Technology In 2 Placements With Various Settings Based On A TerraMap. No Manual Editing Has Been Applied.


TerraLand

     - 1 4000 x 4000 Photorealistic Fully Flat Terrain
     - 9 High Resolution Satellite Images (3 "4096 x 4096" & 6 "2048 x 2048")


Note: Elements That Are Not Listed In Features Such As Clouds, Sky, Sun And etc. Are Not Generated By TerraLand, TerraTrees Or TerraCity.
